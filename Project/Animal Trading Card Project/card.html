Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth. Their legs alone are taller than many humans about 6 feet.

Scientific Name: Giraffa
Average Length: Male: 5-6 m, F: 4.6m
Average Lifespan: 25 years
Habitat: Grassland, Savana

A giraffe's neck is too short to reach the ground. As a result, it has to awkwardly spread its front legs or kneel to reach the ground for a drink of water.Giraffes only need to drink once every few days. Most of their water comes from all the plants they eat.Giraffes spend most of their lives standing up.
